About the DASS-21

The Depression, Anxiety, and Stress Scale (DASS-21) is a 21-question psychological assessment designed to measure the severity of core symptoms of depression, anxiety, and stress.

By answering a series of statements, you'll receive insights into your current mental health state. Remember, this is a screening tool and not a clinical diagnosis.
